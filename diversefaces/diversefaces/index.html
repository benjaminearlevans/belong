Diverse Faces is a collection of (free to use) images that make it easy to bring some diversity into user experience design. We created Diverse Faces to help ensure that the sites we see represent the world we live in. We're launching soon, and would love to have you o board.

Want to take part? Please contact Belong or come join us on Twitter if you'd like to see more diversity in tech!
